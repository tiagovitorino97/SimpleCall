[size=4][b]What Is It?[/b][/size]
Simple Call lets you [b]call a dealer to come meet you[/b] from the phone. The dealer walks or runs to your location, waits a bit, then goes back to their routine. If you go inside, they'll wait at your last outdoor spot and text you or give up after a set time if you don't come out.

[size=4][b]Features[/b][/size]
[list]
[*][b]One-button summon[/b] from the Dealer Management phone app
[*][b]Pathfinding[/b]: dealer walks or runs to you
[*][b]Indoor fallback[/b]: dealer waits at the door if you go inside; texts you and leaves after a timeout if you don't come out
[*][b]Meet delay[/b]: choose how long they stay before leaving
[*][b]Early leave[/b]: interact with the dealer during the wait and they leave right away
[*][b]Dealer messages[/b]: optional texts "On my way", "Here at the door", "Leaving"
[*][b]No double-calling[/b]: can't call the same dealer again while they're already on the way
[*][b]Multiple dealers[/b]: call different dealers at the same time
[/list]
